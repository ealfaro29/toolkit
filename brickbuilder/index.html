Core Controls

  • Select & Move: Click to select. Drag to move. Items snap to grid automatically.
  • Multi-Select: Hold Shift and click multiple items, or drag a box to marquee select.
  • Pan Canvas: Hold Space and drag, or press H to toggle Hand mode.
  • Zoom: Use mouse wheel or + / - keys. 0 resets.
  • Context Menu: Right-click any brick for quick actions (Flip, Rotate, Color).

Pro Techniques

  • Precise Coloring: Select multiple bricks, then pick a color to apply it to the entire selection instantly.
  • Smart Groups: Grouped items move together and maintain their relative layers. Useful for creating complex sub-assemblies.
  • Dimension Control: Use the Properties Panel input fields to key in exact width/height values for pixel-perfect resizing.
  • Layer Management: Objects getting hidden? Use "Bring to Front" or "Send to Back" in the property panel or context menu.
